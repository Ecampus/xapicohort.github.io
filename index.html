Who sponsors this?

TorranceLearning has hosted the xAPI Learning Cohorts since the Fall 2015 Cohort, following the model set forth by ADL's community-building efforts. Our commitment to providing a free, vendor-neutral space for learning about xAPI continues to present day, hosting two 12-week Cohorts and two free day-long conferences each year as well as this site.
